Privacy Policy for Stremini AI

Last Updated: January 2025

What We Do

Stremini AI protects you from scams and fraud by analyzing screen content when YOU choose to scan.

Data Collection

We DO NOT collect, store, or share:

  • Screen content
  • Personal information
  • User activity logs
  • Any data from your device

Accessibility Service Usage

Our accessibility service is used ONLY to:

  • Read screen text when you manually activate the scanner
  • Detect phishing and scam attempts
  • Display warning tags near suspicious content

The service NEVER:

  • Runs automatically in the background
  • Records your screen
  • Accesses your screen without your permission
  • Sends data to external servers (except for AI analysis during scanning)

How to Control Data

You have full control:

  • Scanner only works when YOU activate it
  • Disable accessibility service anytime in Settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all functionality

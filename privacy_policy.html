Privacy Policy for Stremini AI

Last Updated: January 2025

TL;DR: Stremini AI only scans your screen when YOU activate it. We don't collect, store, or share any of your data. You have complete control.

What We Do

Stremini AI protects you from scams and fraud by analyzing screen content when YOU choose to scan. Our AI-powered system detects phishing attempts, suspicious links, and fraudulent messages to keep you safe.

Data Collection

We DO NOT collect, store, or share:

  • Screen content (it's analyzed in real-time and immediately discarded)
  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • User activity logs or browsing history
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Any data from your device

Accessibility Service Usage

Our accessibility service is used ONLY to:

  • Read visible screen text when you manually tap the scanner button
  • Detect phishing and scam attempts in real-time
  • Display warning tags near suspicious content on your screen
  • Protect you from emotional manipulation and urgency tactics

What We NEVER Do

The accessibility service NEVER:

  • Runs automatically in the background without your action
  • Records your screen or takes screenshots
  • Accesses your screen without your explicit permission
  • Monitors your activity when the scanner is not active
  • Stores any screen content on your device or our servers
  • Shares data with third parties or advertisers

How AI Analysis Works

When you activate the scanner:

  1. Screen text is read from visible content only
  2. Text is sent securely (HTTPS encrypted) to our AI service for analysis
  3. AI analyzes for scam patterns and threats
  4. Results are displayed as warning tags on your screen
  5. All data is immediately discarded after analysis (not stored)

How to Control Data

You have full control:

  • Manual activation: Scanner only works when YOU tap the scanner button
  • Disable anytime: Turn off accessibility service in Settings → Accessibility
  • Uninstall: Remove the app completely to remove all functionality
  • No background activity: App does nothing when not actively used

Data Security

  • All communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No data is stored on our servers
  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No tracking or analytics installed

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

  • Cloudflare Workers: Hosts our AI analysis backend
    • Screen text is sent for analysis only when you scan
    • Data is processed and immediately discarded
    • No logs or storage of your data

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. We recommend parental guidance for young users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store any personal data, there is no data to:

  • Request access to
  • Request deletion of
  • Request correction of
  • Request portability of

You maintain complete control by simply not using the scanner feature or uninstalling the app.
